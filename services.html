FAQ

Common questions,
honest answers

Is there a minimum contract period?

No lock-in contracts. We offer month-to-month agreements for recurring services. One-off and event cleans are always available with no commitment required.

Are your staff insured?

Yes. All Saigon Clean staff carry full liability insurance. We are also covered for accidental damage during service, so your property and contents are protected.

Do you supply cleaning products and equipment?

We supply all products and professional-grade equipment. Our products are eco-safe, non-toxic, and approved for use in hospitality and food-service environments.

Can you clean outside of business hours?

Absolutely. We offer early-morning, evening, and weekend scheduling. Most of our commercial clients prefer after-hours or before-open cleans to avoid disruption.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for 24 hours notice to reschedule or cancel a session without charge. For recurring contracts, a 7-day notice is required to pause or end service.

Which districts do you cover?

We cover all districts in Ho Chi Minh City including Quận 1, 2, 3, 4, 7, Bình Thạnh, Thủ Đức, and Phú Mỹ Hưng. Contact us for outlying areas — we'll do our best to accommodate.
